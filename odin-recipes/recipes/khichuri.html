Khichuri

Khichuri is a traditional Indian dish made from a combination of lentils and rice, cooked together in a single pot. It is simple, nutritious, and often served with pickles, chutney, and a side of spicy sauce. Perfect for a comforting meal!

Ingredients

  • Rice

  • Lentils

  • Water or vegetable broth

  • Salt to taste

  • Ghee or oil

  • Ghee or oil

  • Pickles and Chutney

Steps

  1. Cook the rice and lentils together in a pot.
  2. Stir in the spices like cumin and turmeric.
  3. Cook until the mixture is soft and fully cooked.
  4. Serve with pickles and chutnry.
