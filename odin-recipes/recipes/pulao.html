Pulao

Pulao is a fragrant and flavorful rice dish cooked with spices, herbs, and sometimes meat or vegetables, commonly enjoyed in South Asian cuisine.

  • Rice
  • Spices
  • Herbs
  • Oil or ghee
  • Water or broth
  • Protein

Steps

  1. Rinse and soak rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Saut√© onions, garlic, and ginger in oil or ghee.
  3. Add spices and mix well.
  4. Add drained rice and stir.
  5. Pour in water (1.5x rice volume) and cook.
  6. Cover and simmer until done.
  7. Fluff and serve.
